What is Joyful Events

A traditional wedding in which steeped in customs and rituals are passed down through generations These weddings often take place in religious settings, with formal attire and a solemn atmosphere. The bride walks down the aisle accompanied by her father, while the groom waits at the altar. Vows are exchanged, rings are placed on fingers, and the couple shares their first kiss as married partners.
